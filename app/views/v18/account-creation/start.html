Use this service to:

  • get authorisation for existing and new heat networks
  • apply for a license to upgrade or build new heat networks
  • amend information about any heat networks you've already told us about
  • submit mandatory reporting information on your organisation's heat networks

Who this service is for

You must use this service if your organisation undertakes regulated activities on a heat network as a:

  • heat supplier
  • network operator
  • developer
  • third party acting on behalf of any of these
Help with 'regulated activities on a heat network'
If you're not sure about whether or not your organisation is responsible for a heat network, or whether its activities need to be regulated, see the heat network guidance.

How to access the service

Continue to sign up or sign in and we'll guide you through the process.

If you need to sign up for the first time, you must use your individual work email address. Do not use a shared mailbox email address. If you need to provide team access to the service, you’ll be able to invite team members individually after you’ve signed in.

If your organisation has never used this service before

When prompted, you’ll also need to create an organisation account with Ofgem.

You must be your organisation’s ‘regulatory contact’ to do this.
Help with 'regulatory contact'

This is your organisation’s most senior member of staff responsible for heat networks.

As regulatory contact, you'll:

  • be Ofgem’s main point of contact for any regulatory and compliance matters
  • have full access to all features in the account, for example managing users and submitting heat network information
  • be initially responsible for inviting other users into the account and setting their permissions

You can change the regulatory contact in the future, if you need to.
