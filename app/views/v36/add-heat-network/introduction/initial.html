Add a heat network - introduction

{{title}}

To help us understand your specific obligations, we need some further information from you. We'll provide definitions and guidance for any possibly unfamiliar concepts.

You'll first need to tell us:

  • which regulated activities your organisation undertakes on the heat network - operation, supply or both
  • whether the network meets the definition of a ‘shared ground loop’ (SGL) heat network
  • if an SGL heat network, whether it’s owned, operated by and serves one group of individuals, and - if not - whether consumers are charged a fixed fee to access it
  • which country the heat network's consumers are in
  • what type of heat network it is, and how much of it your organisation is responsible for, including buildings and energy centres
  • whether the heat network is currently providing heating, cooling or hot water to consumers, and - if so - whether the organisation began a regulated activity on or after 1 April 2025
  • if an SGL heat network, a postcode that covers the network’s approximate location
  • if a district network, whether the heat network is a 'self-supply' network
  • whether any other suppliers sell to consumers on the heat network
  • whether the heat network supplies heating, cooling or hot water to any other organisations or heat networks
