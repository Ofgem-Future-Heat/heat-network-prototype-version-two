Add a heat network

A heat network is a system in which heating, cooling or hot water is generated at a central source and supplied to customers through a network serving either multiple buildings or multiple occupants in a single building.

Heat network operation and heat supply are now regulated activities and require authorisation for each heat network. This is obtained by registering your heat network with Ofgem.

If the heat network is not yet operational, it must be registered and authorised by Ofgem before it commences operation and the supply of heat to consumers.
