Assigning a supplier and operator

To comply with heat network regulations, each heat network must have an operator and a supplier.

These roles might not exist at the moment, so assign the person or organisation best suited to each role. The supplier and operator can be the same person or organisation.

Supplier

Suppliers are responsible for supplying heat to end users. They also have overall responsibility for billing customers, even if they outsource this to an agent.

Operator

Operators are responsible for the maintenance of the heat network and its infrastructure.

We recommend assigning someone who already does a similar role. These roles can be done by the same person or company.
