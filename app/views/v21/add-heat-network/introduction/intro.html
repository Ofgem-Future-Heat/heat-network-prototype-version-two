Add a heat network - introduction

{{title}}

Only heat network operators can initiate adding a heat network. After you've added your operational data, you can invite any suppliers to contribute their supply data.

As an operator:

  • You must answer a short series of introductory questions about your heat network.
  • We'll let you know your specific obligations, and what further information we need.
  • After this, you'll be able to save your progress at any point.
  • You must then provide the remaining information for your heat network.
  • If you need other organisations to provide supply data, you can invite them to into the service.
  • You can then submit your information to Ofgem for processing.

What to include

You can combine multiple connected heat networks on a single submission, providing you operate them all.

For instance, where you operate a district heat network that connects to one or more communal networks that you also operate, you can submit data about the district heat network and the connected communal networks together.

We will need to ask for more specific data about any buildings you identify as being connected communal networks.
