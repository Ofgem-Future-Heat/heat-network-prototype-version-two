Help with ‘regulated activity’

Heat supplier

Regulated activity in relation to a heat supplier means providing space or process heating, hot water or cooling to some or all of the end users on a heat network.

Network operator

Regulated activity in relation to a network operator means:

  • owning or operating some or all of the assets on the heat network (for example, pipework, heat exchangers, substations or energy centres) or having significant control over them
  • having ultimate decision-making authority to invest in the network, make upgrades and direct repairs and maintenance to support its continued operation
