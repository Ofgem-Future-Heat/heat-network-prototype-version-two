Add a heat network - introduction

{{title}}

Only heat network operators, or third-parties acting on their behalf, can initiate adding a heat network. After you've added your operational data, you can invite any suppliers to contribute their supply data.

As an operator:

  • You must answer a short series of introductory questions about your heat network.
  • Based on your answers, we'll let you know what further information we need.
  • After this, you'll be able to save your progress at the end of each section of the service.
  • You must then provide the remaining information for your heat network.
  • If you need other organisations to provide supply data, you can invite them into the service.
  • You can then submit your information to Ofgem.
