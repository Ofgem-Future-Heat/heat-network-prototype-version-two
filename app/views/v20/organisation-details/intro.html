When you've finished adding this information, you'll have full access to the service and can start providing data about your heat networks.

Before you start

You'll need information about:

  • whether your organisation trades under another name or from another address
  • your organisation's financial resilience
  • the start and end dates of your organisation's financial year
  • whether the organisation is part of a joint venture, special purpose vehicle or is a wholly owned subsidiary of another organisation, and if so the details of any parent organisations
What questions are asked about financial resilience?

You may need to ask your accounts department for some of the financial informatiom we need.

We'll ask whether your organisation has financial accounts for the last financial period of 12 consecutive months. If it does, we need to know:

  • the net profit or loss for the most recent financial year
  • the total value of liquid assets that the organisation controls or has unrestricted access to
  • whether the organisation's assets exceeded its liabilities at the end of the most recent financial year
  • what the average monthly fixed costs were for the organisation's heat networks for the previous financial year
  • what the average monthly fixed income was for the organisation's heat networks for the previous financial year
  • whether the organisation is satisfied it can meet its costs as they fall due within the next 12 months
  • whether the organisation hedges its gas or electricity requirements, and if so, what percentage volume of costs, and for how many months ahead
