Add a heat network - introduction

{{title}}

To help us understand your specific obligations, you'll first need to tell us:

  • which regulated activities your organisation undertakes on the heat network - operation, supply or both
  • whether the heat network uses a shared ground loop
  • what type of heat network it is, and how much of it your organisation is responsible for, including buildings and energy centres
  • whether the heat network is currently providing heating, cooling or hot water to consumers, and - if so - when this began
  • if a district network, whether the heat network is a 'self-supply' network
  • whether the heat network buys thermal energy from or supplies thermal energy to another heat network

We'll provide definitions and guidance for any possibly unfamiliar concepts as you progress.
