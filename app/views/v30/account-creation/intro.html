You can create an account for your organisation if you’re the ‘regulatory contact’ for heat networks.

If somebody else is the regulatory contact, ask them to create the organisation’s account instead. Once it’s set up, the regulatory contact can invite you to access the account.

If you do not wish to continue, you can close this browser. No information will be saved.

Help with 'regulatory contact'

This is your organisation’s most senior member of staff responsible for heat networks.

The regulatory contact:

  • is Ofgem’s main point of contact for any regulatory and compliance matters
  • has full access to all features in the heat network service, for example managing users and submitting heat network information
  • is initially responsible for inviting other users into the service and setting their permissions

The regulatory contact for an organisation can be changed if necessary.
