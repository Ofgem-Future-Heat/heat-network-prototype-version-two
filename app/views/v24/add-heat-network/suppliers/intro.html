You’ve told us that this heat network has one or more other suppliers on it.

You’ll need to add each supplier to this network so that we can invite them to add their supply data.

  • Find the supplier from a list of those already in our system.
  • If you cannot find them in the list, ask them offline to create an account with us and come back when they’ve done this.
  • Confirm which supplier to proceed with and indicate which buildings they supply to.
  • Repeat until every building on the heat network has an allocated supplier.
  • Confirm your final list of suppliers.
  • After you submit your heat network information to Ofgem, we’ll send an invite to each supplier, asking them to add their supply data
You will not be able to register your heat network with us until all buildings have an allocated supplier.
