Use this service to:

  • get authorisation for existing and new heat networks
  • apply for a license to upgrade or build new heat networks
  • amend information about any heat networks you've already told us about
  • submit mandatory reporting information on your organisation's heat networks

Who this service is for

You must use this service if your organisation undertakes regulated activities on a heat network as a:

  • heat supplier
  • network operator
  • developer
  • third party acting on behalf of any of these
Help with 'regulated activities on a heat network'
If you're not sure about whether or not your organisation is responsible for a heat network, or whether its activities need to be regulated, see the heat network guidance.

How to access the service

You'll need:

  • a GOV One Login account
  • an Ofgem account for your organisation
  • permission to use the heat network service on behalf of your organisation

If you have all this, you can sign in with GOV One Login.

If your organisation does not have an account with Ofgem

We'll guide you through the process after you've signed in with GOV One Login.

If you don't have permission to use the heat network service

We'll show you how to request access. The first person at your organisation to gain permission will need to be the organisation's 'regulatory contact' for heat networks. For limited companies, this must be a director or company secretary listed on the Companies House register.
