Use this service to:

  • get authorisation for existing and new heat networks
  • apply for a license to upgrade or build new heat networks
  • amend information about any heat networks you've already told us about
  • submit mandatory reporting information on your organisation's heat networks

Who this service is for

You must use this service if your organisation undertakes regulated activities on a heat network as a:

  • heat supplier
  • network operator
  • developer
  • third party acting on behalf of any of these
Help with 'regulated activities on a heat network'
If you're not sure about whether or not your organisation is responsible for a heat network, or whether its activities need to be regulated, see the heat network guidance.

How to sign in

If your organisation is not set up to use the heat network service

You'll need to sign in with GOV.One Login, then create a heat network account for your organisation.

The person who creates the account must:

  • have the legal authority to act on behalf of the business
  • agree to be the business's 'regulatory contact' for the scheme

A 'regulatory contact' is Ofgem's first point of contact should we ever need to speak to someone about the account.

If your organisation is already set up to use the service

Unless you're the regulatory contact, you'll need an invitation to use the service, either from your organisation's regulatory contact or someone with user management privileges.

If you've already been invited to use the service

You can sign in to the service via GOV One Login, using the email address that the invitation was sent to.

If you're not sure if you or your organisation are set up to use the service

Continue with GOV One Login. We'll provide signposting along the way to let you know what to do.
